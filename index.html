Dalgona Coffee

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Instant coffee 2 tbsp.
Sugar 2 tbsp.
Hot water 2 tbsp.
Milk 2 cups
Tapioca pearls 1/2 cup

Step 1: In a bowl, combine the instant coffee, sugar, and hot water. Mix with a whisk, stand mixer, or hand mixer until the mixture becomes thick, light brown, and fluffy.

Step 2: Put 1/4 cup of cooked tapioca pearls into each glass.

Step 3: Pour 1 cup of milk into each glass.

Step 4: Carefully top off the glass with the coffee cream mixture.

Step 5: Mix the coffee cream into the milk with a straw or a spoon and enjoy!

Banana Bread

  • Time: 90 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Baking soda 1 tsp.
Salt 1/4 tsp.
Butter 1/2 cup
Brown sugar 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Mashed overripe banana 2 1/3 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

Step 2: In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.

Step 3: Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

Step 4: Slice and enjoy!

Dark Chocolate Granola

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Oats 2 cups
Cocoa powder 1/4 cup
Cinnamon 1 tbsp.
Nut butter 1/2 cup
Honey 1/4 cup
Chocolate chips 1/2 cup

Step 1: In a bowl, combine oats, cinnamon, and cocoa powder.

Step 2: Add nut butter and honey to the mixture. Combine evenly.

Step 3: Add chocolate chips and combine evenly.

Step 4: Press mixture into a baking tray or pan.

Step 5: Bake at 300 F for 20 minutes.

Step 6: Let it cool fully and break into pieces.

Coffee Boba

  • Time: ~
  • Serves: ~
Ingredients Quantity
Instant coffee 1 tbsp.
Sugar 2 tbsp.
Tapioca starch 1 tbsp.
Water 1/8 cup
Tapioca starch 1 cup

Step 1: In a bowl, combine the instant coffee, sugar, tablespoon of tapioca starch, and water.

Step 2: Heat the mixture until it becomes thick.

Step 3: Add the remaining cup of tapioca starch and knead until a dough forms.

Step 4: Roll the dough into a snake. Cut small segments and roll them into balls.

Step 5: Cover the balls in tapioca starch and sift out the excess powder.

Step 6: Boil for 8 minutes and drain out the water before serving.

Fruit Boba

  • Time: ~
  • Serves: ~
Ingredients Quantity
Fruit 2 oz.
Sugar 1 tbsp.
Tapioca starch 1 tbsp.
Water 1/4 cup
Tapioca starch 1 1/4 cup

Step 1: Blend the fruit, sugar, water, and scoop of tapioca starch in a blender or food processor until there are no clumps.

Step 2: Strain the mixture.

Step 3: Cook on medium-low heat until the mixture thickens. Add the remaining tapioca starch and mix/knead to form a dough.

Step 4: Roll the dough into a snake. Cut small segments and roll them into balls.

Step 5: Cover the balls in tapioca starch and sift out the excess powder.

Step 6: Boil for 8 minutes and drain out the water before serving.

Froggy Boba

  • Time: ~
  • Serves: ~
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 2 tbsp.
Water 2 tbsp.
Tapioca starch 1 tbsp.
Green food coloring a few drops
Tapioca starch 1/2 cup
Matcha powder 1 tbsp.
Black sesame seeds 2 tbsp.

Step 1: In a bowl, combine the sugar, water, tablespoon of tapioca starch, and food coloring.

Step 2: Cook on medium-low heat until the mixture thickens. Add the remaining tapioca starch and matcha powder and mix/knead to form a dough.

Step 3: Shape the dough into small frog shapes, with one larger circle at the bottom and 2 smaller ones at the top (like Mickey Mouse).

Step 4: To make eyes, put a sesame seed in the center of each small circle.

Step 5: Cover the frogs in tapioca starch and sift out the excess powder.

Step 6: Boil for 8 minutes and drain out the water before serving.

Meringue Cookies

  • Time: 75 minutes
  • Serves: ~
Ingredients Quantity
Egg whites 4
Vanilla extract 3/4 tsp.
Salt 1/8 tsp.
Sugar 3/4 cup
Cornstarch 2 tsp.

Step 1: Preheat oven to 225F, and line baking tray with parchment paper. Place tip inside of piping bag, and set aside.

Step 2: Place egg whites, vanilla and salt in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment. Whisk on high speed until soft peaks form.

Step 3: In a small bowl, mix together sugar and cornstarch.

Step 4: Turn mixer onto medium speed, and slowly add the sugar mixture, pouring down the side of the mixer bowl.

Step 5: Scrape bowl, and return mixer to high speed and continue to mix until meringue has hard peaks and a glossy, shiny appearance.

Step 6: Spoon meringue into the bag. Pipe small 1.5″ cookies onto the lined baking tray.

Step 7: Place tray into preheated oven, and bake for 60 minutes. Once the hour is up, turn oven off and let meringues continue to dry out for an additional hour. Remove from oven after the second hour, and allow meringues to cool completely on a cooling rack.

Onion Biscuits

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: ~
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 80 g
Powdered sugar 40 g
Oil 40 g
Chopped onion 30 g
Flour 200 g
Salt 3 g
Whole milk 60 g

Step 1: Cream the butter and sugar.

Step 2: Add oil and mix until fully combined.

Step 3: Add milk and mix until fully combined.

Step 4: Add onion and mix until fully combined.

Step 5: Preheat oven to 350 F. Sift the flour and salt.

Step 6: Throughly mix the wet and dry ingredients.

Step 7: Flatten balls of cookie dough onto a tray. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.

Lemon Mochi Tarts

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Whole milk 1/2 cup
Sugar 1/2 cup
Butter 1 tbsp.
Lemon juice 2 tbsp.
Mochiko 1/2 cup
Baking powder 1/4 tsp.
Yellow food coloring a few drops

Step 1: Heat the milk and sugar in a saucepan until the sugar is dissolved.

Step 2: Add butter and yellow food coloring, then pour the mixture into a bowl.

Step 3: Add the lemon juice and gradually add the rice flour while mixing. Then add baking powder and mix until smooth.

Step 4: Grease and flour 4 muffin tins. Pour the mixture into the tins.

Step 5: Bake at 350 F for 15 minutes until the edges are golden.

Step 6: Scrape the edges with a knife when done and let cool.

Matcha Lava Cake

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
White chocolate 1 cup
Oil 1 tbsp.
Matcha powder 1 tbsp.
Dark chocolate 1/2 cup
Butter 1/4 cup
Egg 1
Egg yolk 1
Sugar 2 tbsp.
Flour 2 tsp.

Step 1: Melt together white chocolate, oil, and sifted matcha powder. Pour into molds and refridgerate for 15 min.

Step 2: Melt together dark chocolate and butter.

Step 3: In a separate bowl, mix together the eggs and sugar.

Step 4: Pour the egg mixture into the chocolate mixture and combine. Add flour.

Step 5: To assemble: pour the batter into ramekins, add matcha chocolate on top, and cover with more batter.

Step 6: Bake at 450 F for 8 minutes.
